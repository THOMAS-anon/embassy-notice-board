Document Authentication & Legalization

Documents from Ethiopia (for use in China)

  • **Process:** Notarization (Ethiopia) → Authentication (Ethiopian MoFA) → Legalization (Chinese Embassy in Ethiopia) → Submit at Ethiopian Embassy in Beijing.
  • **Fee (Individual):** 317.70 RMB (Cash only)
  • **Fee (Commercial):** 331.82 RMB (Cash only)
  • **Processing Time:** 10 minutes (upon arrival)

Documents from China (for use in Ethiopia)

  • **Process:** Notarization (Chinese Notary) → Authentication (Chinese MoFA/FAO) → Submit at Ethiopian Embassy in Beijing.
  • **Fee (Individual):** 508.32 RMB (Cash only)
  • **Fee (Commercial):** 543.62 RMB (Cash only)
  • **Processing Time:** 10 minutes (upon arrival)

Immigration & Citizenship Services

Online applications available via Digital Invea or Ethiopian Passport Services.

New Passport for Children

Parents/guardians must apply in person. Requires birth certificate (authenticated), parent's passports (original + copy), and 2 passport photos (3x4 cm) of the child.

Fee: $200 USD (Cash only)

Lost/Stolen/Damaged Passport

Requires copy of lost passport (or number), police report (English/certified translation), and 2 photos. Fee is $450 USD. Second loss is $450 + $150 USD.

Fee: $450 USD (Cash only)

Laissez-Passer (Emergency Travel)

For children (needs authenticated birth cert + parent passports) or adults (needs police report + copy of lost passport).

Fee: $120 USD (Cash only)

Vital Events Registration

Birth Certificate Registration

Requires original hospital birth certificate, passports of both parents, and 2 photos of the child. Must be registered within 90 days of birth.

Fee: $30 USD (Cash only)

Marriage Certificate Registration

Requires both spouses to be present, proof of unmarried status (verified), identification, photos, and four (4) witnesses (legally residing in China, with ID).

Fee: $30 USD (Cash only) | Processing: 2 hours

Death & Divorce Certificate Registration

Death requires original death certificate and deceased's passport copy. Divorce requires original, authenticated divorce certificate from court.

Death Fee: $20 USD | Divorce Fee: $30 USD (Cash only)

Power of Attorney (PoA) Service

Step-by-Step PoA Process (Grantor in Beijing)

  • **Visit Embassy:** Grantor brings original passport (+ photocopy) and a copy of the Attorney-in-fact's ID (e.g., Ethiopian National ID).
  • **Receive Document:** PoA is processed and received. **Fee: 331.80 RMB (Cash only).** Processing Time: 15 minutes.
  • **Send to Ethiopia:** Grantor mails the original hard copy to the Attorney-in-fact.
  • **Final Authentication in Ethiopia:** Recipient must authenticate the document at the Ethiopian MoFA Consular Affairs Directorate General, and then at the Federal Documents Office Authentication and Registration Service (DARS).

Police Clearance & Fingerprint Certification

Process Overview

  • **Visit Embassy:** Applicant brings original passport (+ photocopy) and five (5) photos (3x4 cm). Fingerprints will be taken.
  • **Receive Sealed Envelope:** The signed document and photos are placed in a **sealed envelope** and stamped for security. **DO NOT OPEN THIS ENVELOPE.**
  • **Fee:** Free of charge. Processing Time: 20 minutes.
  • **Processing in Ethiopia:** Mail the sealed envelope to the Federal Police Criminal Investigation Sector - Forensic Investigation Directorate (Only they can open it).
  • **Final Authentication:** After police clearance, the document must be verified by the Ethiopian MoFA and then legalized by the Chinese Embassy in Ethiopia.
